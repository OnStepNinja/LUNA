## The Arduino Parallel

This policy follows a similar model to Arduino, with one important difference:
- The **software** is free to use and redistribute (binary form, MIT License) — **source code is not publicly available**
- The **hardware** design is open and free to build
- The **brand name** is protected — clones are OK, but must not claim to be official
