【EN】About the AI Auto-Learning Mechanism

In the LUNA system, users do not need to read through thick manuals or technical specifications
. This is because LUNA is equipped with a mechanism that directly and automatically transfers the necessary knowledge to Claude AI upon connection.
Automatic Knowledge Delivery: Upon connection, LUNA executes tools such as prompts/get luna_guide or get_lua_guide to deliver design documents containing API specifications and communication protocols to Claude
.
Claude as an Expert: As a result, Claude instantly becomes an "expert" on LUNA, understanding how to perform setups and generate Lua scripts
.
Natural Language Instructions: Users do not need to know technical details; they can simply hand over device manuals (PDFs, etc.) to Claude and describe what they want to do in plain language
.
The documents starting with [Ref] are essentially the knowledge base installed into Claude's "brain." While they are listed as references, all operations and troubleshooting can be carried out intuitively through conversation with Claude
.
